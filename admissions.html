Our Admissions Process

Step 1: Inquiry & Tour

Contact our admissions office to ask questions or schedule a tour of our campus to experience our community firsthand.

Step 2: Application

Download and complete the application form. Submit it along with the required documents to our school office.

Step 3: Assessment

Prospective students will be invited for a friendly, age-appropriate assessment to understand their academic level.

Step 4: Offer & Acceptance

Successful applicants will receive an official offer of admission. Secure your child's place by completing the acceptance process.
