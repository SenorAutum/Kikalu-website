Our Heritage & Vision

A legacy of community education, evolving for the future.

Rooted in Community, Growing for Tomorrow

Established as a District Education Board (DEB) institution, Kikalu School has a long-standing history of providing quality education to our community. We have grown from a primary day school into a robust institution offering both Primary and Junior Secondary School (JSS) education, complete with modern Day and Boarding facilities. Our history drives our commitment to excellence and accessibility for all.

Our Mission

To deliver a balanced and technologically-enhanced curriculum in a supportive environment, nurturing students into competent, confident, and responsible citizens.

Our Vision

To be a leading educational institution recognized for academic excellence and holistic development, empowering rural students to compete and succeed on a national stage.
