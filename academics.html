Our Academic Programs

A seamless educational path from Primary to Junior Secondary.

Primary School (CBC Curriculum)

Our Primary section fully embraces the Competency-Based Curriculum (CBC), focusing on developing pupils' skills and talents through hands-on learning. We build a strong foundation in all key learning areas to prepare them for future academic success.

Junior Secondary School (JSS)

As a registered JSS center, we provide a smooth transition for learners into this critical phase. Our program offers a broad range of subjects, focusing on exploration, critical thinking, and career-readiness to prepare students for their senior school pathways.

Day & Boarding Facilities

Day Scholar Program

Our day program is perfect for local students, providing a full day of learning and activities in a structured environment.

Boarding Program

Our boarding facilities offer a secure and caring environment with supervised studies and a vibrant student life.

Extracurriculars

We offer a rich program of sports, clubs, and societies to ensure the holistic development of every student.
